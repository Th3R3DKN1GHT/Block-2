ABOUT US

The purpose of the Sunrise Bar is to fulfill the nutritional needs of a human in a day with just 2 bars. Sunrise bar was created by Silvana Felix, Sofia Mouriño, Victoria Gutierrez, Andres Sotomayor and Regina Adame with the purpose of helping with the second Sustainable Development Goal of the United Nations, Zero Hunger.

Our mision is to not only reduce world hunger, but to also give families suffering from it a sense of security and protection.

“Hunger is not a problem. It is an obscenity. How wonderful it is that nobody need wait a single moment before starting to improve the world.”

—Anne Frank
